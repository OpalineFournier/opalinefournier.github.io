Between the Lines

Book Donations

The Book Donation Initiative at The Literary Canvas involves fundraising for a cause. We donate new books to teens who don't have access through fundraising, book drives, and partnerships.

Video Essays

Bringing awareness through the power of words

Video essays are a way to connect, even if you aren't a couple subway stops distance. The Video Essay initiative prompts learning, social justice, and the collaboration of ideas.

Book Club

Discussion groups at The Literary Canvas involve in person meetings for high schoolers and middle schoolers to connect, collaborate, and feel heard. Running book-club style, these meetings will include workshops as well as engaging discussions.
